A Terraria mod that brings more water guns to the game that actually do damage

Adds:
26 water guns each with their uniqueness
11 new ammo types for water guns
1 town npc

Water Guns consume bottled water as ammo, and it could be bought from the new Swimmer NPC
Water Guns are pumped over-time automaically, gaining new powerful attacks

This mod is still work-in-progress
More items, enhancments and better sprites are comming
