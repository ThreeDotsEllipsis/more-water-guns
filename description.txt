A Terraria mod that brings more water guns to the game that actually do damage

Adds:
26 water guns each with their uniqueness
11 new ammo types for water guns
7 accessories to boost water guns

Water Guns consume bottled water as ammo, and it could be bought from the Merchant NPC

(WIP) You can pump the Water Guns by right-clicking to make them stronger, at full pump level they gain new powerful attacks

This mod is still work-in-progress
More items, enhancments and better sprites are comming
